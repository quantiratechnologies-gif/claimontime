Frequently Asked Questions

How do I file a cashless claim?

To file a cashless claim, you must visit a network hospital. Present your health card at the insurance desk. They will fill a pre-authorization form and send it to us. Once approved, the hospital will proceed with your treatment without you paying cash for covered expenses.

What documents are required for reimbursement?

You will generally need: Original Hospital Bills, Payment Receipts, Discharge Summary, Doctor's Prescriptions, Pharmacy Bills, and Investigation Reports. You can upload scanned copies via our portal in the "Claims" section.

Can I add a dependent to my active policy?

Yes, for Retail policies you can add dependents at the time of renewal. For Corporate policies, you can usually add a spouse or newborn within 30 days of marriage or birth by navigating to the "Policies" section and clicking "Modify Dependents".
